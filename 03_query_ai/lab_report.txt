## Summary

This report analyzes a sample of the 2022 Census Economic data focused on the agricultural support activities sector in the United States. The dataset comprises 20 records detailing various industries under the North American Industry Classification System (NAICS), including the number of establishments (ESTAB), total receipts (RCPTOT), and employment (EMP).

## Key Insights

- **Top Industry**: The "Support Activities for Agriculture and Forestry" (NAICS 115) is the broadest category, featuring a high number of establishments (13,724) and substantial total receipts ($34,945,402).
- **Employment Levels**: The industry employs a total of 246,854 individuals, highlighting its significance in the agricultural labor market.
- **Revenue Variations**: Total receipts vary widely across subcategories; for example, "Postharvest Crop Activities (except Cotton Ginning)" (NAICS 115114) shows receipts of $11,091,847, whereas "Cotton Ginning" (NAICS 115111) totals just $2,041,258.
- **Establishment Count**: The number of establishments in specialized activities such as "Farm Labor Contractors and Crew Leaders" (NAICS 115115) is significant at 1,542.

## Recommendation

This data could be utilized to inform policymakers and stakeholders about the economic impact of agricultural support industries and guide initiatives for workforce development in this sector.
